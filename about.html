Who We Are

Central Texas's Carnival Ride and Event Rental Source

Austin Carnival Rides connects Austin TX families, schools, HOAs, churches and businesses with the best carnival ride and party rental equipment in Central Texas. Every bounce house, water slide, obstacle course, carnival game and dunk tank booked through this site is provided and operated by Jump Around Party Rentals - a fully licensed and insured party rental company serving Austin and all of Central Texas.

All rentals are provided by Jump Around Party Rentals, who is fully licensed and insured in Travis County and Williamson County, Texas. Jump Around Party Rentals meets all Texas Department of Insurance requirements for Class B Amusement Ride operators, carrying a minimum of $1,000,000 commercial general liability coverage per occurrence and maintaining a current Texas Amusement Ride Compliance Sticker on every unit.

Jump Around Party Rentals operates from 3616 Bass Loop, Round Rock TX 78665, serving Austin and all of Central Texas for over 14 years. The service area covers Austin ISD, Round Rock ISD, Cedar Park ISD, Leander ISD, Pflugerville ISD and Hutto ISD, as well as all Travis County and Williamson County communities.

Austin Carnival Rides specializes in school carnivals, field days and PTA fundraisers - supplying obstacle courses, bounce houses, carnival games and dunk tanks to school campuses across Central Texas. Certificates of insurance naming school districts as additional insured are available for all Austin area districts upon request.
