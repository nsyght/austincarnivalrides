School Carnival FAQ - Austin TX

What carnival rentals do you provide for Austin TX school events?
Austin Carnival Rides provides obstacle courses, bounce houses, water slides, carnival games, dunk tanks and concession machines for school field days, PTA fundraisers and school carnivals across Austin TX. All rentals are provided by Jump Around Party Rentals, fully licensed and insured in Travis County and Williamson County, Texas.
How many carnival games should I rent for my Austin school event?
For most Austin TX school carnivals with 200-500 students, 6-10 game stations are recommended. Larger events with 500-1000+ students benefit from 10-15 stations plus multiple inflatables. Contact us and we'll help you design the right mix for your campus.
Do you provide certificates of insurance for Austin ISD schools?
Yes. Jump Around Party Rentals provides certificates of insurance for Austin ISD, Round Rock ISD, Cedar Park ISD, Leander ISD, Pflugerville ISD and Hutto ISD campuses. Call (737) 204-8026 to request a certificate for your campus event.
How far ahead should we book school carnival rentals?
Spring school carnival season (April through June) books 4-6 weeks in advance across Austin TX. Book as early as possible for May and June dates, which are the busiest weeks of the school year.
