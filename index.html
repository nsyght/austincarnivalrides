What Is Austin Carnival Rides?

Austin TX's Carnival Ride and Mechanical Attraction Rental Source

Austin Carnival Rides is Central Texas's source for carnival rides and mechanical attractions. This is not a standard bounce house company. Austin Carnival Rides specializes in the full range of portable amusement rides that make an event feel like a real carnival - mechanical bulls, swing rides, bungee trampolines, rock climbing walls, trackless trains, bumper cars, Ninja Warrior courses, orbit rides, giant fiberglass slides and LED mini golf.

All rides are provided and operated by Jump Around Party Rentals, a fully licensed and insured party rental company based at 3616 Bass Loop, Round Rock TX 78665. Jump Around Party Rentals meets all Texas Department of Insurance requirements for Class B Amusement Ride operators, carrying minimum $1,000,000 commercial liability coverage per occurrence and maintaining a current Texas Amusement Ride Compliance Sticker on every unit. Mechanical rides are operated by trained Jump Around Party Rentals staff at your event.

Austin Carnival Rides delivers to Austin, Cedar Park, Round Rock, Georgetown, Pflugerville, Leander and Hutto - and serves Austin ISD, Round Rock ISD, Cedar Park ISD, Leander ISD, Pflugerville ISD and Hutto ISD school carnivals with certificates of insurance available for all campuses. Bounce houses, water slides and obstacle courses are also available as part of complete event packages.

Mechanical bull rentals in Austin TX start at $1,095 for a standard mechanical bull and $1,195 for an American mechanical bull. All mechanical bull rentals include staff operation by trained Jump Around Party Rentals crew, delivery, setup and teardown. Call (737) 204-8026 for current availability.

Yes. Mechanical rides including mechanical bulls, swing rides, bungee trampolines, bumper cars and other motorized attractions are operated by trained Jump Around Party Rentals staff at your Austin TX event. All rentals are provided by Jump Around Party Rentals, fully licensed and insured in Travis County and Williamson County, Texas.
What carnival rides are best for Austin TX school carnivals?
The top rides for Austin TX school carnivals are the Ninja Warrior Challenge, bungee trampolines, rock climbing walls, mechanical bulls and trackless trains. For 200-500 students, 4-6 ride stations are recommended. See school event packages.
Can you rent a trackless train in Austin TX?
Yes. Austin Carnival Rides provides electric trackless trains (4 passenger cars) and standard trackless trains for Austin TX community events, school carnivals and holiday festivals. Starting at $1,095. Call (737) 204-8026 for availability.
What is the most popular carnival ride in Austin TX?
The mechanical bull is the most requested crowd-stopper at Austin TX school carnivals, corporate picnics and rodeo events. The Ninja Warrior Challenge and bungee trampolines are the top school field day picks. The 65ft fiberglass super slide is the most dramatic single-attraction centerpiece at Austin TX festivals.
Do you deliver carnival rides to Austin TX parks?
Yes. Austin Carnival Rides delivers to Zilker Park, Mueller Park and public venues across Travis County and Williamson County. Public park setups may require a City of Austin permit. Jump Around Party Rentals provides certificates of insurance for permitted events upon request.
Are carnival rides safe for Austin TX school events?
